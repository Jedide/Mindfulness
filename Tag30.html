Day 30

Reflection
Congratulations! This is your last day. You made it through all the small tasks. The last task will be to reflect on the last thirty days. How do you feel now? Could you cope easier with unnessecary thoughts? Did you enjoy the benefits of doing things mindfully? The important thing now is, not to stop. The benefits of doing things mindfully and live in awareness are coming slow and need steady training. You are free to do the 30 Days Challenge over and over until you are naturally being aware in all those topics and beyond. Alternative just catch up with new challenges or Mindfulness meditaion. Thank you for your consistency.
