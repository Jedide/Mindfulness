Day 1

Mindful walking
Througout today, be aware of walking. Try as often as possible to focus on your feet while walking. If you catch yourself thinking and lose focus, simply focus on your feet again. Be aware of the felling of your feet and the sensation of the ground while pressing your feet against it. Don't worry if it is hard in the beginning and you lose focus. Everytime you can think of your task today, is a win.
